Composite Objects and Association

Class Relationships
  • Composition - an object consist of several sub-objects
  • Association - one object is associated with other objects
  • Inheritance - related objects (see later)
Composition
  • HAS A
  • a computer - has a screen, keyboard, moudse, disk etc
  • an employee - has a name, works id, location etc
  • a rectangle - defined by topLeft, bottomRight Point objects
Association
  • USES A
  • a driver - is associated with a car
  • a husband - is associated with a wife
